## 书籍 (Books)

* 《反对爱》(Against Love), 作者 Laura Kipnis (New York: Pantheon Books, 2003).
* 《爱的解剖：单偶制、通奸和离婚的自然史》(Anatomy of Love: The Natural History of Monogamy, Adultery and Divorce), 作者 Helen Fisher (W. W. Norton, 1992).
* 《超越单偶制：婚姻中性替代方案的近期研究》(Beyond Monogamy: Recent Studies of Sexual Alternatives in Marriage), 编辑 James R. Smith 和 Lynn G. Smith (Johns Hopkins University Press, 1974).
* 《双性恋和同性恋丈夫：他们的故事，他们的话语》(Bisexual and Gay Husbands: Their Stories, Their Words), 编辑 Fritz Klein 和 Thomas Schwartz (Binghamton, N.Y.: Harrington Park Press, 2001).
* 《打破欲望的障碍：多重关系的新方法》(Breaking the Barriers to Desire: New Approaches to Multiple Relationships), 编辑 Kevin Lano 和 Claire Parry (Five Leaves Publications, 1995).
* 《共喜：利用嫉妒作为通往无条件爱之路的冥想》(Compersion: Meditations on Using Jealousy as a Path to Unconditional Love) (电子书), 作者 Deborah Anapol (lovewithoutlimits.com, 2005).
* 《同性伴侣的遗产规划》(Estate Planning for Same-Sex Couples), 作者 Joan M. Burda (American Bar Association, 2004).
* 《道德浪女：无限性可能的指南》(The Ethical Slut: A Guide to Infinite Sexual Possibilities), 作者 Dossie Easton 和 Catherine A. Liszt (Oakland, Calif.: Greenery Press, 1998).
* 《爱的未来：亲密关系中灵魂的力量》(The Future of Love: The Power of the Soul in Intimate Relationships), 作者 Daphne Rose Kingma (Broadway Books, 1998).
* 《群体婚姻：当代多边婚姻研究》(Group Marriage: A Study of Contemporary Multilateral Marriage), 作者 Larry L. Constantine 和 Joan M. Constantine (Macmillan Publishing Company, 1973).
* 《男女同性恋伴侣法律指南》(A Legal Guide for Lesbian & Gay Couples), 作者 Denis Clifford, Frederick Hertz, 和 Emily Doskow (Nolo, 2007).
* 《女同性恋多边恋读本：开放关系、非单偶制和随意性行为》(The Lesbian Polyamory Reader: Open Relationships, Non-Monogamy, and Casual Sex), 编辑 Marcia Munson 和 Judith P. Stelboum (Binghamton, N.Y.: Haworth Press, 1999).
* 《女同性恋多边忠贞：为心向多重并发性爱开放的女性准备的快乐指南》(Lesbian Polyfidelity: A Pleasure Guide for the Woman Whose Heart Is Open to Multiple, Concurrent Sexualoves), 作者 Celeste West (San Francisco: Booklegger Publishing, 1996).
* 《这种生活方式：换偶者色情仪式一瞥》(The Lifestyle: A Look at the Erotic Rites of Swingers), 作者 Terry Gould (Richmond Hill, Ontario: Firefly Books, 2000).
* 《生活在一起：未婚伴侣法律指南》(Living Together: A Legal Guide for Unmarried Couples), 作者 Ralph Warner, Toni Ihara, 和 Frederick Hertz (Nolo, 2006).
* 《更多爱：多边忠贞入门》(Loving More: The Polyfidelity Primer), 作者 Ryam Nearing (Pep Publishing, 1992).
* 《囚禁中的交配：调和情色与家庭》(Mating in Captivity: Reconciling the Erotic and the Domestic), 作者 Esther Perel (HarperCollins, 2006).
* 《没有婚姻的金钱：未婚伴侣的财务安全指南》(Money Without Matrimony: The Unmarried Couple’s Guide to Financial Security), 作者 Sheryl Garrett 和 Debra A. Neiman (Kaplan Business, 2005).
* 《单偶制的神话：动物和人类的忠诚与不忠》(The Myth of Monogamy: Fidelity and Infidelity in Animals and People), 第二版, 作者 David P. Barash 和 Judith Eve Lipton (Owl Books/Henry Holt, 2002).
* 《这种新的亲密关系：开放式婚姻和另类生活方式》(The New Intimacy: Open-Ended Marriage and Alternative Lifestyles), 作者 Ronald Mazur (Lincoln, Neb.: iUniverse, 2000).
* 《非暴力沟通：一种生命的语言》(Nonviolent Communication: A Language of Life), 作者 Marshall B. Rosenberg (Encinitas, Calif.: PuddleDancer Press, 2003).
* 《开放式婚姻：一种新的配偶生活方式》(Open Marriage: A New Life Style for Couples), 作者 Nena O’Neill 和 George O’Neill (M. Evans and Company, Inc., 1984).
* 《异教徒多边恋：成为心灵的部落》(Pagan Polyamory: Becoming a Tribe of Hearts), 作者 Raven Kaldera (Woodbury, Minn.: Llewellyn Publications, 2005).
* 《多重爱：双性恋和多边生活的设计》(Plural Loves: Designs for Bi and Poly Living), 编辑 Serena Anderlini-D’Onofrio (Binghamton, N.Y.: Harrington Park Press, 2004).
* 《多边恋：给无知者和充满希望者的路线图》(Polyamory: Roadmaps for the Clueless & Hopeful), 作者 Anthony D. Ravenscroft (Crookston, Minn.: Crossquarter Publishing Group, 2004).
* 《多边恋：这种没有界限的新爱：可持续亲密关系的秘密》(Polyamory: The New Love Without Limits: Secrets of Sustainable Intimate Relationships), 作者 Deborah M. Anapol (San Rafael, Calif.: Intinet Resource Center, 1997).
* 《娱乐性爱：换偶生活方式内幕指南》(Recreational Sex: An Insider’s Guide to the Swinging Lifestyle), 作者 Patti Thomas (Cleveland, Ohio: Peppermint Publishing Company, 1997).
* 《重新定义我们的关系：负责任开放关系指南》(Redefining Our Relationships: Guidelines For Responsible Open Relationships) 作者 Wendy-O Matik (Oakland, Calif.: Defiant Times Press, 2002).
* 《性与爱手册：多边恋！双性恋！换偶者！灵性！（甚至）单偶制！一本实用的乐观关系指南》(The Sex and Love Handbook: Polyamory! Bisexuality! Swingers! Spirituality! (& Even) Monogamy! A Practical Optimistic Relationship Guide), 作者 Kris A. Heinlein 和 Rozz M. Heinlein (Do Things Records & Publishing, 2004).
* 《被挑出的单身：单身人士如何被刻板印象化、污名化和忽视，却依然从此幸福地生活》(Singled Out: How Singles are Stereotyped, Stigmatized, and Ignored, and Still Live Happily Ever After), 作者 Bella DePaulo (St. Martin’s Press, 2006).
* 《单身国情咨文：单身女性畅谈生活、爱情和追求幸福的状态》(Single State of the Union: Single Women Speak Out on the State of Life, Love, and the Pursuit of Happiness), 编辑 Diane Mapes (Seal Press, 2007).
* 《单身：满足、充实和独立的艺术》(Single: The Art of Being Satisfied, Fulfilled and Independent), 作者 Judy Ford (Adams Media Corporation, 2004).
* 《交换俱乐部：当代性风俗研究》(Swap Clubs: A Study in Contemporary Sexual Mores), 作者 William 和 Jerrye Breedlove (Sherbourne Press, 1964).
* 《初学者换偶：这种生活方式介绍》(Swinging for Beginners: An Introduction to the Lifestyle), 作者 Kaye Bellemeade (New Tradition Books, 2003).
* 《换偶：真实换偶者的真实故事》(Swinging: Real Stories by Real Swingers), 作者 Terry O’Day (Charleston, S.C.: BookSurge Publishing, 2005).
* 《三个人恋爱中：从古代到现代的三人行》(Three In Love: Ménages à Trois from Ancient to Modern Times), 作者 Barbara Foster, Michael Foster, 和 Letha Hadady (Backinprint.com, 2000).
* 《三人行手册：与三人睡觉的实用指南》(The Threesome Handbook: A Practical Guide to Sleeping with Three), 作者 Vicki Vantoch (New York: Thunder’s Mouth Press, 2007).
* 《未婚互许：作为未婚伴侣共同生活的基本指南》(Unmarried to Each Other: The Essential Guide to Living Together as an Unmarried Couple), 作者 Dorian Solot 和 Marshall Miller (Marlowe & Company, 2002).
* 《我们要恋爱：浪漫之爱的本质与化学反应》(Why We Love: The Nature and Chemistry of Romantic Love), 作者 Helen Fisher (Owl Books, 2004).
